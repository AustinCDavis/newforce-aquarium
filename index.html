Martin's Story

You may not be able to tell from the image, but that's me casting the net. The 3 time sexiest man alive, FBI special agent, two time nobel prize winner, and avid fish collector. Only thing is only one of these are true and you'll never guess which one... All jokes aside I fell in love with fish at a young age working with my dad on his fishing boat. I began to love the feeling of reeling in a new fish or a new record weight fish. This feeling led me to start harvesting and collecting the fish for my aquarium. I chose to do this so other people too could appreciate the creativity and beauty of nature that I've been blessed to experience. So shown below may not be my prettiest fish or biggest fish, but they were my most memorable and exhilarating catches. Come view the full collection with me and feel free to ask questions!

- Best catches, Martin.

Tanzania

Popular for is beautiful scenerey, big game hunting, and extreme fishing! If you visit Tanzania be open to the wonderful and diverse cultures and experiences!

Thailand

As a country of Southeast Asia, Thailand, is one of the world's most renowned fishing locations. Having over 2,300 different species including our friend the betta.

Amazon River

Many know the Amazon river and its mountains to be unforgiving. While this is true, there is such an immense ecosystem of underwater life. Given its size it is no wonder so many species call it home.

Mali

Tread with caution. I don't advise going to Mali unless you are an experience traveler and have plans made well in advance. Mali is known for its crime, terrorism, and kidnappings. My hobbies take me to the remote regions so with a trusted guide I was able to safely enter and leave the country.

Hawaii

The island that needs little explaining. I believe everyone has dreamed of going or has gone to Hawaii at least once. The island's reputation of beauty, culture, peace, and fun are accurate. In addition, they welcome a host of exotic wildlife that only add to its beauty. Also, to my surprise, plenty of wild chickens.
